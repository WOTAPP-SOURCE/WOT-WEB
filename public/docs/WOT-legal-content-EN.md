### What is a cookie?
A cookie is a small text file stored on your device when you visit a website. It allows the site to function properly and, in some cases, to measure its audience.

### Cookies used on this site
- **Strictly necessary cookies.** Essential for the site to function (storing your preferences, including your cookie choice). They do not require your consent and cannot be disabled.
- **Analytics cookie (Google Analytics).** We use Google Analytics to understand how the site is used (pages viewed, visit duration). The corresponding cookie (`_ga` and related cookies) is set **only after your explicit consent** via the cookie banner. If you decline, no analytics cookie is set.

### What we do not do
To date, this site sets **no advertising, remarketing or marketing-targeting cookies**. We do not share your browsing data with advertising networks.

### Managing your consent
You can accept or decline analytics cookies at any time. To change your choice, use the "Cookies" link in the footer, which re-displays the consent banner. You can also delete cookies from your browser settings.
